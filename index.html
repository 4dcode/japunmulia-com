When clients have high-level requirements or use cases for a blockchain idea/platform/application, we can help them to analyse the requirements, break them down to more specific lower level requirements. Decentralized Applications

Known as a decentralized application, or 'DAPP' for short, the concept has been one of the more novel ideas to emerge from the blockchain community. Armed with self-executing smart contracts, proponents of the technology have envisioned ways to replace everything that today requires a centralized leadership, from businesses and solutions to governments.
